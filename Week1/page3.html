Being the graduating class of 2020 came with a lot of mixed feelings for me.
I absolutely loved being at home. Some people say they can’t stay home for long but I can stay forever, honestly.
Why anyone wants to leave is a mystery to me.

The only real thing that I missed was prom which is truly surprising for an introvert like me.
The fact that my social battery lasted for a few hours the first time I went to the dance was unbelievable.
But honestly, I had so much fun then and I wanted to go again for the last time.

And it never happened. Now, I can’t say that I’m upset about the reason prom was canceled but missing out on senior prom was a bit sad.
It’s probably the most over-glorified event in history.
Okay, that’s clearly an exaggeration but think about it.
Was your high school experience anything like the popular Disney original movie series High School Musical?
I didn’t think so...
